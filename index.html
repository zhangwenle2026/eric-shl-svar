Read Aloud 朗读练习

💡 Key Tips 关键技巧：
• Read at a natural pace — not too fast, not too slow 用自然的语速朗读——不要太快也不要太慢

Listening 听力理解

3 dialogues × 5 questions · 3 段⭐真题对话 × 5 道推理题

💡 Strategy 听力策略（对话版）：
• Listen for opinions and attitudes, not just facts 注意观点和态度，不只是事实
• Pay attention to tone and implication 注意语气和言外之意
• Inference questions: "What can you infer?" = 需要推理，答案不会直接说出来
• Listen twice — first for main idea, second for details 听两遍——第一遍抓大意，第二遍抓细节
🎧 Dialogue 对话 1 · Office Relocation 办公室搬迁
📌 Key Points to Listen For 听力要点：
• Man's positive point 男方优点: closer to train station 离地铁更近
• Woman's concern 女方担忧: noise in open-plan 开放式办公噪音
• Unknown factor 不确定因素: number of quiet rooms 安静房间数量
• Missing facility 缺少设施: no cafeteria 没有食堂
🎧 Dialogue 对话 2 · Project Deadline 项目截止日期
📌 Key Points to Listen For 听力要点：
• Client pressure 客户压力: board wants to see progress 董事会要看进展
• Current status 当前状态: core 80% done, behind on testing 核心80%完成，测试落后
• Proposed solution 方案: phased delivery 分阶段交付
• Resource needed 所需资源: extra developer for 2 weeks 额外1名开发2周
🎧 Dialogue 对话 3 · ⭐真题 Christmas Party Planning 圣诞派对筹备
📌 Key Points to Listen For 听力要点：
• Party timing 派对时间: 3 days before Dec 25 (Wednesday) 12月25日前3天（周三）
• Division of work 分工: Justin = catering 餐饮, Tina = decorations + invitations 装饰+邀请
• Committee 委员会: No more people needed 不需要更多人
• Justin's personality: doesn't like dancing, prefers mediator role 不喜欢跳舞，想当调解人
🎧 Dialogue 对话 4 · ⭐真题 Vending Machine Supplier 自动售货机供应商
📌 Key Points to Listen For 听力要点：
• Boss can't attend 老板无法参加: asks Sarah to go instead 让Sarah代替参加
• Task 任务: take notes + negotiate better price 记笔记+谈更好价格
• Requirements 要求: ≥5 beverage options, ≤$10,000, no compromise on quality 至少5种饮料选项，不超过1万美元，不牺牲质量
• Cannot close deal 不能当场签约: wants to consider other suppliers 要考虑其他供应商
🎧 Dialogue 对话 5 · ⭐真题 GTBfly Airways 航空公司协助
📌 Key Points to Listen For 听力要点：
• Father's details 父亲信息: John Matthew, 70 yrs old, flight 223, July 12th, seat A3 70岁，223航班，7月12日，座位A3
• Health conditions 健康状况: diabetic + recent knee surgery 糖尿病+膝盖术后
• Prescription rule 处方规定: must carry prescription, otherwise meds go to checked baggage 必须带处方，否则药品放托运
• Email confirmation 邮件确认: won't include seat details — confirmed at check-in 不含座位详情——值机时确认
